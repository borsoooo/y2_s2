About Me

Hi, I’m Orsolya Holló, originally from Hungary and currently living in the Netherlands for almost two years. I’m studying Creative Business at Breda University of Applied Sciences, a program that perfectly blends the two things I’m most passionate about: creativity and marketing.

My heart beats for marketing—especially when it connects with my other lifelong love: sports. I’ve played handball for nearly 12 years and still play today, however not on a professional level anymore. I’ve also did athletics, basketball, football, and volleyball—because standing still just isn’t in my nature.

Beyond sports and studies, I’m someone who thrives on being productive. I currently have four side jobs, and while that might sound intense, it reflects who I am: hardworking, disciplined, and driven. I grew up watching my parents work incredibly hard to give me and my siblings the best opportunities—and I’ve carried that work ethic with me ever since.

My Story

I come from one of the poorest regions of Hungary, from a tiny village with less than 300 people. We didn’t have an elementary school there, so I traveled to a nearby village each day to attend school. After that, I continued my studies at Zrínyi Ilona High School in Miskolc, where I followed a bilingual English program.

From early on, I knew that learning English was my way out—my key to something bigger. My dream was to study abroad, and I worked hard during high school to make that dream a reality. That was the time I made the tough decision to quit handball, in order to focus on my grades and part-time work to save money.

I graduated with one of the best grades in my class, and earned both academic and sports honors. In 2023, I took the biggest step of my life so far and moved to the Netherlands to begin my studies at Breda University of Applied Sciences.

That move changed everything. My life did a full 180-degree turn, and I’ve grown so much since—personally, academically, and emotionally. I’m proud of where I come from, and even prouder of how far I’ve come.
